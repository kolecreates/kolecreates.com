The vision for Howlo was to revive a proven-popular concept: anonymous geographic-based forums. A hole in the niche market opened after major players (YikYak) made unfortunate mistakes. Howlo needed to be a simple to use, fast, gorgeous-looking, unique-feeling, engaging mobile app. A friend and I built that, with a React Native front-end experience, and Google's Firebase platform for the back-end. At the time, Howlo was the only active social app of its kind in the United States.
